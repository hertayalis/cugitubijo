Julia Fox Officially Declares That Capris Are In for 2024

July 2024 · 2 minute read

If you’re looking to refresh your wardrobe in 2024, allow Julia Fox to inspire your shopping cart.

While celebrating the new year in Miami, the Uncut Gems actor made a head-turning appearance with her daring outfit—which featured a daring pair of capris as the centerpiece. The unique bottoms were nearly entirely covered in a bold mustard-yellow and graphite-gray illustration, and also included maroon and white stripes running down the sides. Fox color-coordinated via a distressed gray corset with two white racer stripes down the center of the bust and a cropped leather moto jacket in a pale yellow.

She upped the ante with the rest of her ensemble’s elements: embellished beige pumps from a Jimmy Choo and Jean Paul Gaultier collaboration and a coordinating sand-colored bag.

305pics//Getty Images

305pics//Getty Images

Fox’s New Year’s Eve party look was relatively low-key, compared to some of her most viral street-style moments in 2023. (Honorable mentions include a minidress seemingly sculpted out of hair, a gauzy see-through clown costume, and even a dry-cleaning garment bag worn as a necklace.) Knowing her, however, we’re sure to get some more eye-popping fashion moments in the coming year.

Fox opened up about her experimental sense of style in a 2022 interview with the Cut. “[My publicity team was] like, ‘Oh, they’re a little too editorial,’‘It’s too sexy. It’s too provocative. It’s too out there,’” she said. “I was just like, ‘Okay, I guess I just have to be more Hollywood and more bland.’ But fuck that narrative if I want to pop out and wear crazy shit—which is what I want to wear.”
