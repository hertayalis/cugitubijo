See Jennifer Lopez Style Her Baggy Denim Jumpsuit with Timberland Boots and a Birkin

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Jennifer Lopez's latest street-style outfit elevates a casual staple with the coolest accessories.

The multi-hyphenate was seen out and about in Los Angeles yesterday, where looked effortlessly cool in an oversized, short-sleeved denim jumpsuit by R13. She paired the baggy coveralls with a classic pair of tan Timberland boots, and her accessories included a luxe gold watch, chunky hoop earrings, and matching gold aviator sunglasses. She completed the look with a brown suede Hermès Birkin bag from her extensive collection, while wearing her honey-blonde hair pilled atop her head in a messy bun.

thecelebrityfinder/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

The pop star has been spotted out and about in some chic monochromatic looks over the past week. On Wednesday, the star channeled a cozy fall vibe in a brown suede miniskirt and a matching jacket, which featured a brown leather collar as well as shiny gold buttons down the center and on the cuffs. She completed the look with a pair of knee-high platform leather boots in a buttery caramel, which perfectly matched her glossy, croc-embossed Birkin.

One day earlier, she paired a clingy turtleneck top in a camel color from Intimissimi—the lingerie brand for which Lopez is a global brand ambassador—with creamy soft cargo pants featuring a wide-leg fit, a high-rise silhouette, and plenty of boxy pockets. She added a pair of tan boots with a thick block heel, a pale brown Hermès Birkin bag, tortoiseshell cat-eye sunglasses, and gold hoop earrings.

The actor spoke with Bazaar earlier this year about her ambassadorship with the lingerie brand. “I feel connected to Intimissimi,” the Hustlers actor said at the time. “They encourage women to feel confident in their skin and, more importantly, to embrace that feeling! The designs reflect that. All of their pieces are elegant, luxe, and wearable. You feel inspired and empowered as soon as you slip it on.”
