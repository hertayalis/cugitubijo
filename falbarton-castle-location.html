Hogwarts Legacy Falbarton Castle Location and How To Get Inside

If you're stuggling to find Falbarton Castle in Hogwarts Legacy, then here's a comprehensive location guide to finding the POI.

First Published February 28, 2023, 21:17

Falbarton Castle is one of the most sought-after POIs in Hogwarts Legacy, as players are required to explore this location to complete The High Keep quest and two Merlin Trials. However, finding Falbarton Castle is a tricky task in itself, especially if you're a newcomer to Hogwarts school. Therefore, some players may have trouble finding this point of interest in the game and get stuck at a certain checkpoint. Therefore, here's everything you need to know about finding Falbarton Castle in Hogwarts Legacy.

Where To Find Falbarton Castle In Hogwarts Legacy

The Falbarton Castle is located in Hogsmeade Valley, specifically in the east of Hogsmeade near the mountain cliffs. Although players can reach this location by foot, the most efficient way is either by teleporting to the nearest waypoint or flying with the Broom to reach Falbarton Castle quickly. You can look around Falbarton Castle but can't go inside until you activate The High Keep main quest.

How To Get Inside Falbarton Castle

To explore Falbarton Castle, you must first meet Natty near the castle and then climb the battlements by following a specific path. Once on the battlements, you need to cast Accio on a crate to clear the path and destroy the wooden planks to enter the room.

Next, you need to open the main gate by using Alohomora on a locked door and then follow the stairs until you reach the roof. To get to the roof, you also need to cast Depulso on a crate and drag it with Levioso to place it on the broken staircase segment.

Finally, you need to follow Natty on Highwing to your destination and unlock Beast Mounts. By following this path, you can explore Falbarton Castle and discover all the secrets it holds.
