Dua Lipa Is Giving Us Disco Fever in Bedazzled Fishnet Unitard

May 2024 Â· 2 minute read

Dua Lipa's on-tour style gets more dazzling by the day.

The singer's Future Nostalgia Tour made a stop at the Osheaga Festival in Montreal. For the performance, Lipa wore a custom semi-sheer black leopard-print jumpsuit from Andrea Adamo. The fishnet unitard featured a cutout at her torso and purple crystals that highlighted the bold pattern. The ensemble also included matching opera gloves and a black bralette with a halter neckline.

As for glam, stylist Jake Gallagher kept Lipa's hair long and straight, parted neatly down the middle. Her glam, done by her tour makeup artist, Shelby Smith, played into her '80s aesthetic with neon pink eye shadow and an intense winged eyeliner. The singer finished the look with a peachy nude lip, silver chain earrings, and black leather booties with pointed toes and block heels.

Lipa listed off some of her most recent tour destinations in the caption, writing, "Last week ~ montreal ~ toronto ~ chicago ~ montreal ~ london."

This is hardly the first notable outfit from the "Levitating" singer's run of shows this year. In June, Lipa wore a different head-turning unitard: a hot pink piece with a subtle floral embellishment and corseted ribbing down the front. The ensemble again included matching opera gloves and heeled boots.

Lipa's tour photographer, Elizabeth Miranda, shared a photo dump on Instagram featuring Polaroids of the singer and her fans. She captioned the post, "Polaroids from tour <3333." Lipa commented back with just five red hearts.
