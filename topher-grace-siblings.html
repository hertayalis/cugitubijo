Who Is Topher Grace's Wife?

Since getting married in 2016, Topher Grace and his wife, Ashley Hinshaw, have kept a low profile regarding their romance.

After one year of dating, the Spider-Man 3 actor proposed, and the pair married vows in Montecito, California, the following year. Hinshaw posted a photo of her wedding shower celebrations on social media weeks before she was married.

There are a few cute aspects of Topher and Ashley's courtship that you should be aware of, even if the pair has mostly kept their private lives under wraps.
