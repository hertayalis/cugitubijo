Dax Tejeras Wife : Meet Veronica Tejera and Dax Tejera Children

June 2024 · 1 minute read

Late ABC producer Dax Tejera, 37, tragically passed away suddenly after suffering a heart attack – and his wife Veronica Tejera has since been arrested for child endangerment after it transpired that the couple left their two young daughters aged two and five months old in a hotel room when they went out for dinner on the night that he died.

Who is Veronica Rocio Bautista?

For those who are unfamiliar with Veronica Rocio Bautista, let us tell you, she is the daughter of Rocio Bautista and José C Bautista, who is a project manager under the Salazar and Associates company.

The 33-year-old’s mother is a school teacher in Howell, New Jersey. Veronica completed her graduation from Penn State.

Earlier, in 2019, Veronica worked as a producer for “CNN Right Now With Brianna Keilar.” She and Dax met back in 2013 in New York with the help of a mutual friend.

Soon afterwards, the two started dating and walked down the aisle on August 3, 2019 at the La Catedral de Santa Catalina de Alejandría in Cartagena, Colombia.
