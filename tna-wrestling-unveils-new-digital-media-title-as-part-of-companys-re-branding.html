TNA Wrestling Unveils New Digital Media Title As Part Of Companys Re-Branding

June 2024 · 2 minute read

Impact Wrestling is now TNA Wrestling once again, and that is coming with brand-new title belts. Now, we have a look at the new Digital Media Title, the very one held by Tommy Dreamer.

TNA Wrestling’s rebranding efforts have continued, and now Tommy Dreamer has a new title belt to carry around. Scott D’Amore was happy to show Dreamer the new title and put it around his waist. D’Amore also spoke about how this is the end of an era, but he start of a new one.

Well, Tommy, the Impact Wrestling era is coming to an end, and it’s time for TNA Wrestling to return. We’re back, baby! And along with it, we’ve got brand-new championship belts. New championship belt for an old wrestler!

Tommy Dreamer is set to defend the title at the Hard to Kill pre-show against Crazzy Steve. We will have to see if he is able to retain that new hardware.

We already saw the new TNA Tag Team Titles. The new X-Division title is also very snazzy, and Chris Sabin is happy to hold it. TNA Wrestling’s Hard to Kill event will be quite a show, as the re-branding efforts continue.

We will have continued coverage of TNA Wrestling’s rebranding efforts, and everything else in the pro wrestling world, here at Ringside News. Keep checking back for more updates as they are made available.
